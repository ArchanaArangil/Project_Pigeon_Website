Our Vision

Project Pigeon is a student-led non-profit dedicated to providing academic support to underprivileged children in our community.

Founded on the belief that every child deserves access to opportunities for success regardless of their socio-economic background, we strive to bridge the gap by offering personalized tutoring services tailored to the unique needs of each student.

  • One-on-one tutoring sessions
  • Group workshops
  • Enrichment programs

Our team of volunteers collaborates closely with Title I schools to identify and support students who may benefit from additional academic assistance. Through our services, we aim to empower these children with the skills, confidence, and resources they need to thrive academically and beyond.
